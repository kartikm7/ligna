Privacy Policy

At Ligna Wealth, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we use and safeguard your data to provide our services and enhance your experience. Please take a moment to read and understand our practices.

  1. Purpose of Data Usage: We will utilize your personal information to manage your account(s), deliver our services, and continuously improve our offerings to you.
  2. Limited Disclosure: We hold your personal information in the highest regard and will not share it with external individuals or entities, except in the following cases:
    • When required by law or regulation.
    • With carefully chosen service providers or agents who adhere to strict confidentiality standards.
    • Individuals involved in the transfer of our rights and responsibilities under your Customer Agreement.
    • When sharing information with a service provider or agent located in another country, we ensure the same level of protection as mandated for data in India, solely for the purpose of delivering the agreed-upon service.
  3. Data Security: We maintain stringent security measures to safeguard your personal information against loss, theft, unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use, or alteration. Our security protocols vary based on the sensitivity of the data, with the highest level of protection afforded to the most sensitive personal information.
  4. Your Information and Third Parties: If you visit our website or access our mobile application, certain usage-related information such as traffic statistics, IP address, date, and time may be collected. Rest assured, we do not share or utilize this information with third parties.
  5. Compliance and Law Enforcement: Ligna Wealth will share collected information when complying with legal obligations or disclosures required by law enforcement authorities or governmental parties.
  6. International Data Sharing: If your information is shared with a service provider or agent in another country, we ensure they adhere to the same protection standards as required in India, and only for the designated service purpose.

We value your trust and aim to be transparent about how we handle your personal information. By continuing to use our services, you acknowledge and consent to the practices outlined in this privacy policy. If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy or data security, please contact us at info@lignawealth.com Please remember that data security and privacy are important to us, and we continuously strive to provide you with the highest standards of service while safeguarding your personal information.
