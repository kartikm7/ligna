About Us

  • Ligna Wealth draws its inspiration from the Corsican language, where "Ligna" translates to "straight line." This concept symbolizes simplicity and transparency, guiding our approach to providing clear and understandable advice to our clients.
  • We are AMFI Registered Mutual Fund Distributor based in Mumbai, founded by Mr. Madhur Gundecha, a highly experienced professional. Our firm specializes in delivering personalized and comprehensive financial solutions, assisting individuals in achieving their financial objectives.
  • Our area of expertise lies in the management of High Net Worth Individuals (HNIs), Ultra High Net Worth Individuals (UHNIs), and Non-Resident Indian (NRI) clients.
  • We are an AMFI Registered Mutual Fund Distributor (ARN-242950) and SEBI Registered Research Analyst (INHO00009375).
