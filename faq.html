Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a minimum capital required to work with you?

No, not at all. We want to help our clients achieve their financial goals. We want to reach as many clients as possible and make a difference to their financial health.

How do you charge and get paid?

We are a AMFI Registered Mutual Fund Distributor. We get paid by the AMCs directly. The indicative charges are mentioned in the disclosure section. Our product selection is based solely on best fit for our client and not on commission that we earn.

How do offer the services?

Our services are completely digital. The process follow is as follows:

  • We would first like to know your current investments to understand the current asset allocation
  • We will try to understand your risk profile
  • We understand the goal and horizon for which you are investing
  • We will propose you products that we feel are suitable for you. The proposal will include the complete details of the product with history of performance for last 10 years
  • On your confirmation, the order will be placed and you will have to approve it and make payment online to the Intermediary. No payment needs to be done to us directly, similarly the redemption is also into your account directly
  • You will have online access to your portfolio on desktop as well as mobile. You can even transaction yourself through this app, if you want.
